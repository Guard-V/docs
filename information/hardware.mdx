## Hypervisor Hardware Specifications

- **CPU**: AMD Ryzen 9 5950x
- **RAM**: 128GB DDR4 ECC (3200MHz)
- **Storage**: 2x 2TB NVMe (RAID 1)
- **Network**: 2x 10G SFP+ Uplink 

### Resource Allocation

Each VDS gets dedicated resources. For every 8GB of RAM, a user receives one full dedicated core. Nothing is oversold or shared. Each VDS plan includes 7 off-site backups to guarantee data safety and redundancy.
